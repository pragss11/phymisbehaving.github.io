How to upload your photo in 3 steps

  1. Go to imgbb.com — it's free, no account needed
  2. Click "Start uploading" and choose your photo from your phone or laptop
  3. After it uploads, click "Copy BBCode" → then choose "Direct link" and copy that URL — it looks like https://i.ibb.co/abc123/photo.jpg

Paste that link into the "Photo URL" field above and hit Save Settings.
